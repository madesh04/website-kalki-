About Wooden Train Set

Our wooden train set is a timeless classic that encourages imaginative play and helps develop fine motor skills, spatial awareness, and problem-solving abilities. The set is designed to be both durable and versatile, allowing children to create countless track configurations.

Set Includes:

  • 1 engine with magnetic connectors
  • 3 train cars with magnetic connectors
  • 20 track pieces (straight, curved, and junction pieces)
  • 1 bridge with supports
  • 4 trees
  • 2 buildings
  • 4 figurines

Benefits for Development:

  • Enhances fine motor skills and hand-eye coordination
  • Develops spatial awareness and logical thinking
  • Encourages imaginative and creative play
  • Promotes problem-solving through track building
  • Introduces basic concepts of engineering and physics

Safety Information

All our wooden toys are designed with your child's safety as our top priority.

Safety Features:

  • Complies with international toy safety standards
  • Non-toxic, water-based paints and finishes
  • Smooth, sanded edges to prevent splinters
  • Sturdy construction for durability
  • Magnetic connectors are securely embedded

Care Instructions:

  • Wipe clean with a damp cloth
  • Do not submerge in water
  • Store in a dry place when not in use
  • Periodically check for loose parts
  • Keep track of small pieces to prevent loss
