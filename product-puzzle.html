Safety Information

All our wooden toys are designed with your child's safety as our top priority.

Safety Features:

  • Complies with international toy safety standards
  • Non-toxic, water-based paints and finishes

Frequently Asked Questions

While this puzzle set is recommended for children 4-6 years old, a 3-year-old with advanced fine motor skills may enjoy it with adult supervision. However, please note that some pieces may be challenging for younger children.

To clean your wooden puzzle, simply wipe it with a slightly damp cloth. Avoid soaking in water or using harsh cleaning chemicals as these can damage the wood and affect the finish. Allow to air dry completely before storing.
