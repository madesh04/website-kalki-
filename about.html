Our Story

Wooden Toys began in 2018 with a simple mission: to create beautiful, safe toys that spark imagination and connect children with nature.

Our founder, Anita Sharma, was searching for plastic-free toys for her daughter when she discovered a community of skilled artisans in rural India who had been crafting wooden toys for generations. Inspired by their craftsmanship and concerned about the environmental impact of plastic toys, she partnered with these artisans to create a sustainable toy company.

Today, we work with over 30 artisan families across India, combining traditional woodworking techniques with modern, child-centered design to create toys that are not only beautiful but also developmentally appropriate and engaging for children.

Every toy we create is a testament to our commitment to quality, sustainability, and the preservation of traditional craftsmanship.

Why We Use Wood, Not Plastic

At Wooden Toys, our choice of wood over plastic is deliberate and values-driven. Here's why we believe wooden toys are better for children and the planet:

Environmentally Sustainable

Wood is a renewable resource. We source our wood from responsibly managed forests and plant two trees for every one we use.

Non-Toxic & Safe

Unlike many plastics that can contain harmful chemicals, our wooden toys are finished with non-toxic, child-safe materials.

Sensory Development

Wood has a natural warmth, weight, and texture that plastic can't replicate, providing rich sensory experiences for developing minds.

Biodegradable

At the end of their (very long) life, our wooden toys naturally decompose, leaving no microplastics behind.
