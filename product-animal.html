About Wooden Animal Puzzle

Our wooden animal puzzle is designed to introduce young children to different animals while developing essential cognitive and motor skills. Each animal piece has a small knob for easy grasping, making it perfect for toddlers and preschoolers.

Features:

  • 8 chunky animal pieces with easy-grip knobs
  • Vibrant, non-toxic colors that help with visual recognition
  • Smooth, rounded edges for safe handling
  • Sturdy wooden base with animal-shaped cutouts
  • Compact size perfect for small hands and tabletop play

Benefits for Development:

  • Enhances fine motor skills and hand-eye coordination
  • Develops shape recognition and matching abilities
  • Introduces animal names and sounds
  • Encourages problem-solving and logical thinking
  • Builds confidence through successful completion

Safety Information

All our wooden toys are designed with your child's safety as our top priority.

Safety Features:

  • Complies with international toy safety standards
  • Non-toxic, water-based paints and finishes
  • Smooth, sanded edges to prevent splinters
  • Chunky pieces designed to prevent choking hazards
  • Sturdy construction for durability

Care Instructions:

  • Wipe clean with a damp cloth
  • Do not submerge in water
  • Store in a dry place when not in use
  • Keep all pieces together in the puzzle board when not in use
  • Periodically check for loose parts or damage
