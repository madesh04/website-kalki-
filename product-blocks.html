Frequently Asked Questions

Our wooden building blocks are recommended for children aged 2 years and older. While the blocks themselves are sturdy and safe, they may pose a choking hazard for children under 2 years. If you're looking for toys suitable for younger children, please check our 0-2 years category for age-appropriate options.

Our building blocks are crafted from sustainably sourced neem wood, which is known for its durability and natural antibacterial properties. Neem wood is a traditional material used in Indian craftsmanship and is ideal for children's toys due to its strength and resistance to pests.

To clean the blocks, simply wipe them with a damp cloth. Avoid submerging them in water or using harsh cleaning chemicals, as this may damage the wood or finish. Store the blocks in their cotton bag in a dry place when not in use. Periodically check for any splinters or damage and sand lightly if necessary.

Yes, we do offer larger building block sets. In addition to our standard 24-piece set, we have a 48-piece set and a 72-piece set available.
