Return & Refund Policy

Last Updated: June 1, 2024

1. Return Period

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery. To be eligible for a return, your item must be unused, in the same condition that you received it, and in its original packaging.

2. Return Process

To initiate a return, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact our customer service team at returns@woodentoys.com or call +91 98765 43210.
  2. Provide your order number and the reason for the return.
  3. Our team will guide you through the return process and provide a return authorization number.
  4. Pack the item securely in its original packaging.
  5. Include the return authorization number and your order details.
  6. Ship the item to the address provided by our customer service team.

3. Refunds

Once we receive and inspect your return, we will notify you about the status of your refund.

If approved, your refund will be processed within 5-7 business days. The refund will be credited to your original payment method.

Please note that shipping costs are non-refundable. If you received free shipping on your order, the standard shipping cost will be deducted from your refund.

4. Exchanges

If you need to exchange an item for a different size, color, or product, please follow the return process and place a new order for the desired item.

5. Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery. We will arrange for a replacement or refund at no additional cost to you.

Please provide photos of the damaged or defective item to help us process your claim more efficiently.

6. Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Personalized or custom-made items
  • Items that have been used or show signs of wear
  • Items without original packaging
  • Sale items (unless defective)

2. Shipping Methods & Delivery Times

We offer the following shipping options:

Shipping Method Estimated Delivery Time Cost
Standard Shipping 3-5 business days ₹100
Express Shipping 1-2 business days ₹250
Free Shipping 3-5 business days Free (Orders over ₹1,500)

Please note that these are estimated delivery times and may vary based on your location and other factors.

3. Shipping Coverage

We currently ship to all states and union territories in India. For international shipping, please contact our customer service team for availability and rates.

4. Tracking Information

Once your order ships, you will receive a shipping confirmation email with a tracking number. You can track your order by:
