About Wooden Alphabet Blocks

Our wooden alphabet blocks are a timeless educational toy that has been helping children learn for generations. Each block is carefully crafted and features multiple learning elements on each face, making them perfect for early childhood education.

Features:

  • Complete set of 26 alphabet blocks
  • Each block features uppercase and lowercase letters
  • Corresponding images that start with each letter
  • Numbers and mathematical symbols on some faces
  • Smooth, rounded edges for safe handling
  • Comes in a sturdy storage box with a sliding lid

Benefits for Development:

  • Introduces letter recognition and phonics
  • Develops vocabulary and language skills
  • Encourages counting and basic math concepts
  • Enhances fine motor skills and hand-eye coordination
  • Promotes creative building and spatial awareness
  • Supports both independent and collaborative play

Safety Information

All our wooden toys are designed with your child's safety as our top priority.

Safety Features:

  • Complies with international toy safety standards
  • Non-toxic, water-based paints and finishes
  • Smooth, sanded edges to prevent splinters
  • Blocks sized appropriately to prevent choking hazards (4 × 4 × 4 cm)
  • Sturdy construction for durability

Care Instructions:

  • Wipe clean with a damp cloth
  • Do not submerge in water
  • Store in the provided box when not in use
  • Keep all blocks together to prevent loss
  • Periodically check for wear and tear
