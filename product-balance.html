About Wooden Balance Board

Our wooden balance board is a Montessori-inspired toy that offers endless possibilities for active play and physical development. The curved design allows children to rock, balance, climb, and slide, making it a versatile addition to any playroom.

Features:

  • Crafted from premium hardwood for durability and strength
  • Smooth, curved design with no sharp edges
  • Non-slip surface for safe play
  • Versatile design for multiple play configurations
  • Natural wood finish that showcases the beautiful grain

Benefits for Development:

  • Improves balance, coordination, and spatial awareness
  • Strengthens core muscles and gross motor skills
  • Encourages creative, open-ended play
  • Develops confidence and body awareness
  • Provides a healthy outlet for physical energy

Safety Information

All our wooden toys are designed with your child's safety as our top priority.

Safety Features:

  • Complies with international toy safety standards
  • Non-toxic, water-based finishes
  • Smooth, sanded edges to prevent splinters
  • Sturdy construction for durability
  • Tested to support up to 80 kg of weight

Care Instructions:

  • Wipe clean with a damp cloth
  • Do not leave outdoors or in damp environments
  • Store in a dry place when not in use
  • Periodically check for wear and tear
  • Apply natural wood oil occasionally to maintain finish

Frequently Asked Questions

Our wooden balance board is primarily designed for indoor use. While it can be used outdoors occasionally on dry, clean surfaces, we recommend keeping it indoors to preserve the wood finish and prevent damage from moisture or extreme weather conditions. The natural wood finish is not weatherproofed for permanent outdoor use.

The balance board is recommended for children aged 3 years and older. However, younger children can use it with close adult supervision. The board grows with your child and can be enjoyed well into the elementary school years, with many adults even finding it useful for balance exercises and as a standing desk accessory.

Yes, our balance board is sturdy enough for adult use with a weight capacity of 80 kg. Many parents enjoy using it for yoga, balance exercises, or as a standing desk accessory. To maintain the beautiful wood finish of your balance board, we recommend wiping it clean with a slightly damp cloth as needed.
