Frequently Asked Questions

Shipping Information

We process all orders within 1-2 business days. Standard shipping within India takes 3-5 business days after processing. Express shipping (₹150 extra) takes 1-2 business days. International shipping is available to select countries and typically takes 7-14 business days.

Yes, we ship to select countries internationally. International shipping rates vary by destination and order weight. Please note that international orders may be subject to import duties and taxes, which are the responsibility of the recipient.

Yes! We offer free standard shipping on all orders over ₹1,500 within India. International orders and orders below ₹1,500 will have shipping calculated at checkout based on weight and destination.

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track your package on our website or directly through the courier's website. If you haven't received a tracking number within 3 business days of placing your order, please contact our customer service team.

Returns & Refunds

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for unused items in their original packaging. Custom or personalized items cannot be returned unless they arrive damaged or defective. To initiate a return, please email us at returns@woodentoys.com with your order number and reason for return.

To return a product, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact our customer service team at returns@woodentoys.com with your order number and reason for return
  2. We'll send you a return authorization and shipping instructions
  3. Package the item securely in its original packaging if possible
  4. Ship the item back using the provided return label or your preferred carrier
  5. Once we receive and inspect the return, we'll process your refund

Once we receive your returned item, we'll inspect it and process your refund within 3-5 business days. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method. Please note that it may take an additional 5-10 business days for the refund to appear in your account, depending on your bank or credit card company.

If your item arrives damaged or defective, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery. Please include photos of the damaged item and packaging. We'll arrange for a replacement or refund at no additional cost to you. In some cases, we may ask you to return the damaged item using a prepaid shipping label we provide.

Toy Safety

Yes, all our toys are designed with safety as our top priority. We use non-toxic, child-safe finishes and materials. Our toys comply with international safety standards including EN71 (European Safety Standard) and ASTM F963 (US Safety Standard). Each toy undergoes rigorous testing to ensure it's safe for children.

We use only non-toxic, child-safe finishes on our wooden toys. Depending on the toy, we use food-grade mineral oil, beeswax, or water-based paints and sealants that are free from lead, phthalates, and other harmful chemicals. These finishes are safe even if children put the toys in their mouths.

To clean our wooden toys, wipe them with a damp cloth using mild soap if necessary, then dry immediately with a clean towel. Never soak wooden toys or put them in the dishwasher. For disinfecting, you can use a solution of equal parts water and white vinegar. To maintain the finish, you can occasionally apply a small amount of food-grade mineral oil or beeswax.

We have toys specifically designed for infants, toddlers, preschoolers, and older children. If you're unsure which toy is right for your child's age, please contact us for personalized recommendations.

Yes, many of our toys are designed to grow with your child and remain engaging for years. Our puzzles, building sets, and creative play toys often appeal to older children and even adults.
